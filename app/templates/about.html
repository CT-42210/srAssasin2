About & Help

About Senior Assassin

Senior Assassin is a traditional game played by high school seniors around the country. It's a fun way to create lasting memories during your final year of high school.

Our game is organized by senior class representatives and operates independently from the school. The game is designed to be safe, fair, and fun for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

After successfully eliminating a target, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your game account
  2. Go to your game dashboard
  3. Click on "Submit a Kill"
  4. Fill out the form, including uploading your video evidence
  5. Wait for confirmation from other players

If your team has two members and one is eliminated, your team is still in the game. The remaining player continues to hunt their target. At the start of the next round, the eliminated player will be revived if your team is still alive.

A team is only eliminated when all its members have been eliminated.

If you believe a kill was invalid (e.g., it occurred in a safe zone or using non-approved methods), you can vote to reject the kill confirmation when it comes up for voting.

If there are significant disputes, you can contact the game administrators with your concerns.

If you've forgotten your password, please contact the game administrators to help reset your account.

Include your name, team name, and email address in your request for faster assistance.
